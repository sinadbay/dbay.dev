Apps

Splitzy - Fair Cost

Splitzy makes sharing trip costs simple and fair. Add friends, track expenses, and settle up with minimal hassle — so you can enjoy the journey!

Splitzy makes it easy to share trip expenses with friends. Create a trip, add members, record expenses, and see who owes whom. The app calculates fair balances and reduces the number of payments needed so everyone can settle up quickly. This app allows you to create, share and export your expenses in PDF with your friend by end of your trip.
