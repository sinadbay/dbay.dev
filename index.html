About Me

Welcome! I'm Sina Dibay — a software engineer and mentor by day, and a part-time percussionist by night. My days are filled with code, collaboration, and guiding others in their tech journey, while my evenings are devoted to rhythm, performance, and musical expression. I'm based in the San Francisco Bay Area and always open to creative projects, partnerships, and conversations. Feel free to reach out if you're interested in collaborating — whether it's on music, mentorship, or engineering ideas!
